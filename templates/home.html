Our Story

My Family immigrated from Malaysia and like many others they wanted to pursue a better life. Realizing the potential of Atlantic City, they opened & owned Melaka, a Malaysian & Chinese Restaurant which was located on Tennessee Avenue. Since then we've been proudly serving our neighborhood in Egg Harbor Township where we opened Kylin in 2008. We take pride in our quality and hospitality and want each person to feel like family when they come in. Kylin is simply that, family. Welcome and enjoy!
